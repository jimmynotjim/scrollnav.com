Troubleshooting

If you have any problems using scrollnav, read through the troubleshooting guide, ask a question on Stack Overflow, or report an issue on GitHub.

Debug mode

Issues

Please read and understand the [Contributing Guidelines][4] prior to [opening an issue][2]. Ensuring your issue conforms to the guidelines gives it a better chance I’ll be able to help address it.

Questions

For questions about using scrollnav in your own project, your best bet is to post it to [Stack Overflow][21]. The community there is great at lending a hand and can often respond faster than I can, plus it becomes searchable for future developers who may run into the same question. If you’re still stuck, please feel free to reach out to me to ask for help or clarification, I’m [@jimmynotim on Twitter][22].
